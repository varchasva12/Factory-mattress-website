Mattress Material Comparison

🔵 Memory Foam
Excellent pressure relief and motion isolation. Conforms to your body shape. Best for couples and those with joint pain. Available in SwirlGel cooling variant.
🟡 Re-Bonded / HR Foam
Made from recycled foam — eco-friendly and cost-effective. Firm orthopedic support. Excellent durability. Great entry-level option with real performance.
🟢 Natural Latex
100% organic, hypoallergenic, dust-mite resistant. Naturally breathable with pin-core air channels. Premium choice for health-conscious sleepers. Available in Nirvana & Velvista.
🟣 HR Soft Foam
High Resilience foam with plush surface feel. Doesn't sag like traditional soft foam. Ideal balance of softness, durability and support for those who prefer a gentle surface.
🔴 Pocket Spring
Individual wrapped springs respond independently to body weight. Zero motion transfer — ideal for couples. Good airflow and bounce. Combined with foam layers in Mosaic series.
🟠 PU Latex Foam
Synthetic latex foam — resilient, breathable, and durable. Hypoallergenic properties at a more accessible price point. Featured in Dual Comfort+ and Panache premium series.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I order a mattress in a non-standard size?
+
Absolutely! Customization is our core offering. We manufacture mattresses in any length, width, and height you specify. Whether it's an unusual bed frame, a circular bed, or a hospital cot — just share your measurements and we'll build it exactly.
How long does delivery take?
+
For customers in Greater Noida and Delhi NCR, we offer same-day or next-day delivery. For other locations across India, delivery typically takes 2–5 working days depending on the destination.
What foam types are available and which should I choose?
+
We offer re-bonded foam (budget orthopedic), HR foam, HR Soft foam, soft resilience foam, SwirlGel memory foam, PU latex foam, and 100% natural latex. The best choice depends on your firmness preference, budget, health needs, and sleeping position. Our team is happy to advise you.
Can I visit the factory before placing an order?
+
Yes! We welcome factory visits at our facility in Udyog Vihar Extension, Ecotech-II, Greater Noida. You can see the manufacturing process, test different foam samples, and speak directly with our sleep consultants before deciding.
What warranties do BelBed mattresses come with?
+
Depending on the model, BelBed mattresses carry warranties ranging from 60 months (5 years) to 240 months (20 years). The Snoozer series starts at 60M, Dual Comfort and Smudge at 120M, Panache and Flagship at 180M, and the premium Velvista comes with a 240-month warranty.
Do you supply to hotels, hospitals, and bulk buyers?
+
Yes! We serve hotels, hospitals, hostels, PG accommodations, and corporate buyers regularly. We offer competitive bulk pricing, custom branding options, and consistent quality across large orders. Contact us with your requirement for a dedicated quote.
